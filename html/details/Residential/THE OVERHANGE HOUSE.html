The Overhange House

Location

Ahmedabad, Gujarat

Year

2021

Area

2,800 sq ft

Category

Bharuch, Gujarat

Overview

"The Overhang House" is a modern farmhouse defined by its bold, raw concrete geometry and striking cantilevered volumes that stretch out toward the rolling landscape. Expansive floor-to-ceiling glazing breaks up the heavy structural forms, flooding the interiors with natural light while framing panoramic views of the surrounding agricultural fields. The A bold architectural statement that masterfully utilizes dramatic cantilevers to create deep, sheltered spaces and a striking silhouette.ccents and deep, functional rooflines creates a balanced aesthetic that feels both monumental and deeply rooted in its pastoral setting.

Concept

The Overhang House is defined by its namesake architectural feature—a series of prominent, intersecting planes that provide shade and character. The design prioritizes spatial connectivity, with a central double-height volume that link the social areas of the home. Passive design strategies, including stack ventilation and strategic shading, ensure a comfortable interior climate throughout the year, while the large overhangs protect the facade from the intense tropical sun.
